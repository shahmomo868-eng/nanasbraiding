Our story

Braiding the way Nana taught us.

Nana's Braiding started with a simple belief: hair care is care, full stop. Every style is installed with patience, the right tension, and a scalp-first mindset, so your braids look good the day you leave and stay healthy for weeks after.

Based in Boston, we welcome first-timers and regulars alike — natural textures, all lengths, all ages. Come as you are; leave feeling like yourself.
